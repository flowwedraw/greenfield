Source 
pub struct CompressedEdwardsY(pub [u8; 32]);
Expand description

In “Edwards y” / “Ed25519” format, the curve point (x,y)(x,y)(x,y) is determined by the yyy-coordinate and the sign of xxx.

The first 255 bits of a CompressedEdwardsY represent the yyy-coordinate. The high bit of the 32nd byte gives the sign of xxx.
